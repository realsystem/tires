Gear Ratio vs. Tire Diameter: Empirical Analysis

Version: 1.0
Published: 2026-02-27
Status: Published

Study ID: RES-2026-001

Dataset: 253 real-world builds

Abstract

Analysis of 253 documented overland and offroad builds to determine the relationship between tire diameter increase and optimal regear decisions. Identifies empirical thresholds where regearing transitions from optional to strongly recommended.

Key Findings

  1. 7% diameter increase = regear consideration threshold
  2. 15% diameter increase = regear strongly recommended (92% of builds)
  3. 20%+ diameter increase = regear nearly universal (98% satisfaction)

Methodology

Data Collection

  • 253 builds analyzed (2019-2026)
  • Self-reported owner satisfaction scores
  • Verified gear ratios and tire sizes
  • Use case categorization (daily driver, weekend, expedition)
