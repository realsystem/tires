## Gear Ratio Range by Manufacturer

**Toyota**: 3.31 - 4.70
- Modern vehicles trending toward numerically lower ratios (3.31-3.90) with advanced transmissions
- Older vehicles use higher ratios (4.10-4.70)

**Lexus**: 4.30
- Consistent across GX models

**Jeep**:
- **Wrangler/Gladiator**: 3.21 - 5.13 (wide range for different builds)
- **Cherokee/Grand Cherokee**: 3.45 - 3.73

**Ford**: 3.73 - 4.70
**Ram**: 3.21 - 4.56
**Land Rover**: 3.54 - 3.73
**Suzuki**: 4.62 - 5.12 (highest ratios for smaller engines)
